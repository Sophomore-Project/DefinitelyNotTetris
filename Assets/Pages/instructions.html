The goal: Maneuver the falling pieces into complete horizontal lines to clear them and earn points

beware: The pieces don't stop coming-
If they stack all the way to the ceiling, you lose


Clear lines to earn points and to move on to higher, faster levels!

Fortunately, in addition to your quick thinking, you will have many tools at your disposal:

  • Horizontal Movement: Move the block left and right with the LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys

  • Vertical Movement: Move the block down with the DOWN arrow key

  • Rotate: Rotate the block clockwise 90 degrees by pressing the UP arrow key

  • Hold Block: Save a block for later by pressing the SHIFT key once, press it again when you are ready to use that block

  • Ghost Piece: An image of the current block will be shown that depicts exactly where the block will drop should you stop moving left or right

  • Hard Drop: Move the block all the way to the bottom immediately by pressing the SPACE key

  • Preview: The next 5 upcoming blocks can be seen in a window to the right of the gameboard

Power Up Mode:
In power up mode you will be challenged with various power ups and power "downs" that will either help our hurt you when playing through your tetris journey.
This mode works the same as normal tetris with the levels, increasing speed, and game over when you reach the top but as stated has an extra obstacle to it.

What the colors mean:
PINK: When the light pink square come in contact with an object it will slow you current tetromino and upcoming ones for a total of 15 second
BLACK: This power up is a "bomb". When the bomb hits the already placed tetromino it will take away a 5x5 piece of the grid
RED: For the red power up, when it is placed it will gift you with the I shaped cyan piece. It will also fill your upcoming pieces with them.
